About us

Rooted in Kasma Marar, since 2015

R. S. Public School has been serving the children of Kasma Marar, Khajauli and the surrounding villages of Madhubani since 2015. As an English-medium school covering Play Group to Class VIII, we focus on building strong fundamentals, confident communication, and good discipline — all under a tagline we take seriously:

“We meet the challenges of promoting education”
